About Us

Welcome to PG Buddies! We are dedicated to helping you find the perfect paying guest accommodation that fits your lifestyle and budget. With a wide range of options available in various cities, we ensure that you have access to comfortable, safe, and affordable living spaces.

Our mission is to simplify the process of finding a PG by providing you with detailed information and reviews from other tenants, so you can make an informed decision. Whether you are a student, working professional, or simply looking for a temporary place to stay, PG Buddies is here to assist you every step of the way.
